What is IPTV?

IPTV stands for Internet Protocol Television. It allows you to watch TV through an internet connection instead of via antenna or satellite.

Is using IPTV legal?

Using IPTV is legal if the content being streamed is authorized by the rights holders. Some free or unofficial IPTV services may be illegal.

Which devices are compatible with IPTV?

IPTV can be used on Smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, computers, and Android TV boxes using apps like IPTV Smarters, TiviMate, or VLC.

What internet speed do I need for IPTV?

A stable connection of at least 10 Mbps is recommended to avoid buffering or interruptions during IPTV streaming.
